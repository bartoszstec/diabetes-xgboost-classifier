Diabetes Risk Assessment Tool

This tool provides an initial risk assessment for diabetes based on selected demographic information and blood test results. All input fields are required to ensure accurate evaluation.

Please note that this assessment is intended for screening purposes only and does not constitute a medical diagnosis. If the result indicates an increased risk of diabetes, you are strongly advised to consult a qualified healthcare professional and undergo appropriate medical testing.
